My name is Emory Wynn, I'm 23 years old and a recent graduate of Ontario Tech University (OTU). During my years in high school, I was involved in several clubs including Dance, Concert Band, and Programming Club where I gained a lot of my Java and object-oriented programming knowledge.

As a university student, I was enrolled at OTU in the Game Development and Entrepreneurship program. Here I was able to learn an extensive amount of C++ and C#, and by the second year was even able to complete my own game engine! I was an executive member of the Game Programming Club (GPC) where I got to teach others about C++ and programming tips they may not have known before. I was also an active member of our school's Game Development Society (GDSoc) where students get together and talk about what's going on in the game dev industry and other topics like time management.

If I'm not hanging out with my friends or playing games I'm usually working on a new game project that comes to mind. All-in-all that's me and I hope to hear from you soon!
